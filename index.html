COHORT OVERVIEW

The applied data science course is delivered entirely online so students can participate in continuing education without disrupting their lives. Zahara School offers a 12-week project-based data science program that involves “interactive coding challenges” to offer a more hands-on approach to teaching. The course includes one-on-one coaching from instructors and offers step-by-step training on all the skills students need to be prepared for a career in data science. The course prepares students for careers as Data Scientistists, Data Engineers and Data Analysts on completion.

Section 1: 6 Weeks

Scientific Computing and Python for Data Science

Zahara School of data science offers a 12-week-long data science program with a fixed schedule of evening courses. Courses cover topics such as Python, data science, web development, programming and popular data tools such as Pandas, Numpy, Scipy, Matplotlib and Seaborn. Students will leave the program with a portfolio of over 10 “fun and practical” projects that will help bolster your GitHub profile and set you apart from other candidates in your industry. Students will be required to attend 3 online classes per week hosted by live teachers. The program gives you access to mentors and post-graduation support to help you make your next career move.

Section II: week 7-12

Machine Learning and Statistical Analysis

In Unit II, students develop machine learning and statistical analysis skills through hands-on practice with open-ended investigations of real-world data. Students can expect to work with authentic public data sets from organizations like the NHS, or anonymized data on credit card defaults. This unit has a heavy emphasis on creative use of the tools of data science to solve problems from multiple perspectives.
